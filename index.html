Our Formula (10 Key Points)

  1. Diagnose fast: Interviews + data to map constraints and opportunities.
  2. Pick battles: Prioritize high-leverage initiatives with clear owners.
  3. Set metrics: North-star + leading indicators, reviewed weekly.
  4. Design operating cadence: Planning, stand-ups, reviews, retros that fit your size.
  5. Architect simply: Prefer standard platforms and incremental modernization.
  6. De-risk delivery: Stage gates and thin-slice releases.
  7. Upskill teams: Playbooks, enablement sessions, and paired execution.
  8. Communicate: Stakeholder maps, comms kits, and exec dashboards.
  9. Measure ROI: Baseline → target → realized value tracked in one sheet.
  10. Hand-off cleanly: Ownership plans so wins persist after engagement.
